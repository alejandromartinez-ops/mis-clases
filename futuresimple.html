Future Transport

The future with WILL

We use 'will' to make predictions about future transport and technology.

Affirmative

Subject + will + verb

Example:

People will travel in flying cars.

(La gente viajará en coches voladores)

Contraction: I'll, you'll, we'll...

Negative

Subject + won't + verb

Example:

Trains won't use metal tracks.

(Los trenes no usarán vías de metal)

won't = will not

Question

Will + Subject + verb?

Example:

Will we ride hoverboards?

(¿Montaremos en aerotablas?)

Short answers:

  • Yes, we will.
  • No, we won't.
