Los 2 Rectángulos

Practice the Intro and the Main Body using Conditionals

📨 Your friend's email: Crazy Dreams

"Hi! We are talking about dreams in class today. What would you do if you won a million dollars? And if you could have any superpower, what would you choose? Write back soon!"

1️⃣ El Primer Rectángulo (La Intro)

Say hello, ask how they are, and explain why you are writing.

💡 Hints:
  • How are you doing? / I hope you're ok.
  • I'm writing to you because...

2️⃣ El Segundo Rectángulo ("La Chicha")

Answer the email talking about unlikely situations using the 2nd Conditional.

⚙️ Formula:
If + Past Simple, Subject + would + infinitive

📋 Checklist del Alumno

✨ Ejemplo de Respuesta

Compare your text with this example. It doesn't have to be exactly the same, but the structure and grammar should be similar!

Rectángulo 1 (Intro)

Hi Sarah! How are you doing? I hope you're ok. I am writing to you because of your email about your class and your dreams.

Rectángulo 2 (Main Body)

If I won a million dollars, I would buy a big house for my family. Also, if I could choose a superpower, I would fly everywhere!

📨 Your friend's email: The English Test

"Hi! I feel terrible. I failed my English test last week because I played video games all weekend and I didn't study. Now my parents are angry. What do you think?"

1️⃣ El Primer Rectángulo (La Intro)

Say hello, ask how they are, and explain why you are writing.

💡 Hints:
  • How are you doing? / I hope you're ok.
  • I'm writing to you because...

2️⃣ El Segundo Rectángulo ("La Chicha")

Answer the email talking about past regrets using the 3rd Conditional.

⚙️ Formula:
If + Past Perfect (had/hadn't + V3), Subject + would have + V3

📋 Checklist del Alumno

✨ Ejemplo de Respuesta

Compare your text with this example. It doesn't have to be exactly the same, but the structure and grammar should be similar!

Rectángulo 1 (Intro)

Hi David! How are you doing? I hope you're ok. I am writing to you because of your email about your English test.

Rectángulo 2 (Main Body)

I am sorry to hear that! If you had studied for the test, you would have passed it. Also, if you hadn't played video games all weekend, your parents wouldn't have been angry. Next time, study more!
