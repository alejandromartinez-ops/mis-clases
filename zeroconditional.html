💡 When do we use it?

We use the Zero Conditional to talk about things that are always true. It is perfect for scientific facts, rules, and to explain how technology works!

If + Present Simple
,
Present Simple

Example: If you click this, the video starts.

Present Simple
if
Present Simple

Look! If we start with the result, we DO NOT use a comma.
Example: The video starts if you click this.
