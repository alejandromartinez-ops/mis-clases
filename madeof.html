Grammar Time! ⏰

What is it made of?

Learn how to say what your favorite instruments are made of.

🪵 Wood
⚙️ Metal
🧪 Plastic
🎺 Brass
⚫ Rubber
📦 Cardboard
👑 Gold
🥈 Silver
🪟 Glass
✅
👍

Affirmative

Subject + is / are + made of...
1

Singular

A flute is made of silver.

+2

Plural

Cymbals are made of metal.

❌
👎

Negative

Subject + isn't / aren't + made of...
1

Singular

A violin isn't made of rubber.

+2

Plural

Maracas aren't made of glass.

❓
🤔

Question

Is / Are + Subject + made of...?

Is the flute made of gold?

Yes, it is. No, it isn't.

Are drums made of cardboard?

Yes, they are. No, they aren't.
