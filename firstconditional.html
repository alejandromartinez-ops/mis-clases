💡 When do we use the First Conditional?

We use the First Conditional to talk about possible situations in the future and their consequences. It is perfect for planning a mountain trip!

✅ Affirmative

If + Subject + Present Simple , Subject + will + verb
"If we bring a tent, we will sleep well."

❌ Negative

If + Subject + Present Simple , Subject + won't + verb
"If you don't take a torch, you won't see in the dark."

❓ Interrogative

Will + Subject + verb + if + Subject + Present Simple?
"Will we get lost if we forget the map?"

*Remember: If the sentence starts with "If", we put a comma in the middle. If "if" is in the middle, there is no comma!
