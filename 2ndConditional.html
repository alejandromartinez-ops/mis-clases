Writing Practice

Los 2 Rectángulos 📝

Practice the Intro and the Main Body using Conditionals

Target: 2nd Conditional

Your friend's email: Crazy Dreams

"Hi! We are talking about dreams in class today. What would you do if you won a million dollars? And if you could have any superpower, what would you choose? Write back soon!"

1 El Primer Rectángulo (La Intro)

Say hello, ask how they are, and explain why you are writing.

💡 Hints:
  • How are you doing? / I hope you're ok.
  • I'm writing to you because...

2 El Segundo Rectángulo ("La Chicha")

Answer the email talking about unlikely situations in the present or future using the 2nd Conditional.

2nd Conditional Formula: If + Past Simple, Subject + would + verb infinitive

Checklist del Alumno

Ejemplo de Respuesta (Autocorrección)

Compare your text with this example. It doesn't have to be exactly the same, but the structure and grammar should be similar!

Rectángulo 1 (Intro)

Hi Sarah! How are you doing? I hope you're ok. I am writing to you because of your email about your class and your dreams.

Rectángulo 2 (Main Body - 2nd Conditional)

If I won a million dollars, I would buy a big house for my family. Also, if I could choose a superpower, I would fly everywhere!
